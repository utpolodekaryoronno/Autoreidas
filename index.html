Who We Are:

Ability Beyond Borders (ABB) is an internationally focused disability rights charity run by both disabled and non-disabled people.

We are registered in the UK and operate as a small but strategic organisation strongly committed to improving social justice and substantive equality for disabled people across geographic borders.

ABB has emerged from the work of disability rights activists with over 20 years of experience supporting disabled peopleâ€™s rights and civil society organisations across the Chinese-speaking regions of East Asia.

Our aim is to empower and strengthen the rights of disabled people at all levels, from grassroots activism to engaging with disability policy at the local, national and international levels.

We have an established presence in the Chinese speaking regions of East Asia but work internationally to advance social justice and substantive equality for disabled people. Our activities are all aligned with the international disability rights agenda and the United Nations Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD).
